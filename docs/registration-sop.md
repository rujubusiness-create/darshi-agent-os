Developer Registration SOP

Purpose:
Track and manage all developer registrations for Darshi Realty.

Workflow:

1. Registration email arrives at darshirealty@gmail.com
2. AI identifies developer and intent
3. Registration record created in Supabase
4. Status assigned
5. Next action identified
6. Draft response generated
7. User reviews and sends
8. Follow-ups tracked until approval

Statuses:

- Not Started
- Registration Requested
- Documents Required
- Submitted
- Pending Approval
- Approved
- Rejected
- Renewal Required

Documents:

- Trade License
- RERA Certificate
- VAT Certificate
- Company Profile
